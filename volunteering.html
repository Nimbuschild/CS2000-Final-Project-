Are you passionate about preserving the environment and creating a sustainable future? Join us at Eco Alliance, where we're dedicated to making a positive impact on the planet through community-driven initiatives and eco-conscious practices.


As a volunteer with Eco Alliance, you'll have the opportunity to contribute your skills and enthusiasm to a variety of projects aimed at promoting environmental conservation, raising awareness about pressing ecological issues, and fostering a greener, more sustainable way of life.

Date Activity Description Location
April 5, 2024 Beach Cleanup Volunteers gathered to clean up trash along the coastline, focusing on plastic pollution. Coastal Area, City Beach
May 12, 2024 Tree Planting Participants planted trees in a local park to promote biodiversity and combat climate change. City Park
June 20, 2024 Community Garden Maintenance Volunteers helped maintain a community garden, including weeding and watering plants. Community Garden, Green Street
July 15, 2024 Wildlife Habitat Restoration Restoring natural habitats for wildlife by removing invasive species. Nature Reserve
August 20, 2024 Recycling Drive Collecting recyclable materials from the community to promote recycling. Citywide
September 5, 2024 Environmental Education Workshop Hosting workshops to educate the public on environmental issues and solutions. Community Center
